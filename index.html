Frequently Asked Questions

Is this safe for my house or a local park?

Absolutely. Our foam darts are designed for safe play, and our inflatable barriers are gentle on indoor floors and grass. We include safety eyewear for every player so you can worry less and enjoy more.

What if we lose some of the darts?

Don't worry about it! We include 300 darts and expect some to disappear in the heat of battle. You won't be charged for normal dart loss.

Do I need a big truck to pick up the kit?

Not at all. The entire "Party in a Box" is designed to fit in the trunk of a standard SUV or the back of a van. We keep the logistics simple for you.

What ages are the blasters for?

Our kits are perfect for ages 6 and up. We provide a mix of blasters that are easy for smaller hands to load and fire, plus rapid-fire models for the older kids.

Can we use the kit for a church youth event?

Yes! We love serving local Calhoun County youth groups. The kit is built for 25–30 players, making it a high-impact, low-cost activity for your next fellowship or group event.

How long does the setup take?

With our included high-volume electric pump, you can have all 8 inflatable barriers ready to go in about 15 minutes. It's the fastest battlefield you'll ever build.
